# Demo Narration

Begin with the problem: edge detectors miss tiny/occluded wildlife, but full high-resolution processing wastes compute. Show an easy frame returning through the fast path. Show a hard frame triggering a crop, then the remapped and merged output. Display real route and latency metadata. Finish with the controlled comparison and limitations. Keep the demo under three minutes and never substitute animation for measured results.
