# Research Value Proposition

The project’s value is the combination of three things: a precise edge constraint, a measurable adaptive-compute hypothesis, and implementation across training, inference logic, data improvement, and deployment. The novelty is not “CNN plus transformer.” It is whether a calibrated, bounded route to scene context or local resolution provides a better domain-specific Pareto point than fixed inference.
