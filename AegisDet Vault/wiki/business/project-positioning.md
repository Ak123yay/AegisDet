# Project Positioning

Position AegisDet-Pro as an **independent adaptive edge-perception research project**, not a new generic YOLO brand. The strongest story is the full scientific loop: identify a real constrained-perception problem, lock a strong modern baseline, build an interpretable adaptive prototype, collect hard failures, add only evidence-backed modules, deploy to real runtimes, and publish negative as well as positive results.
