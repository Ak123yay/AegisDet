# Science Fair Angle

A suitable question is: “Does uncertainty-triggered selective crop refinement improve small-animal detection accuracy per unit inference cost compared with a frozen YOLO26n baseline?” Independent variable: adaptive refinement policy. Dependent variables: small-object AP/recall, false positives, p50/p90/p99 latency, and refinement rate. Controls: checkpoint, split, image size, runtime, hardware, and evaluator. Later global-context work can become a second study, not an uncontrolled add-on.
