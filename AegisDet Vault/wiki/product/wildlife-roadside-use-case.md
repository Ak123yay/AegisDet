# Wildlife Roadside Use Case

A fixed roadside or trail camera processes mostly empty or easy frames, with occasional small animals near vegetation or at night. The base detector handles ordinary frames. AegisDet activates additional local processing only when detections are tiny, low-margin, or overlapping. The user receives detections and optional alerts, but the research prototype must not be marketed as collision-prevention or safety-certified technology.
