# Hard Frame User Story

Given a small raccoon near the edge of a low-light frame with moderate confidence, the router records the small-object/uncertainty signals. The crop planner adds bounded context, the detector runs on the crop, local boxes are remapped, and class-aware merging resolves duplicates. The output records the crop count and route reason so the improvement and cost are auditable.
