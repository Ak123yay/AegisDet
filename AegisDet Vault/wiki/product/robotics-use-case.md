# Robotics Use Case

A mobile robot needs low-latency perception while camera distance, motion blur, and clutter change. The same adaptive mechanism could refine a small object without increasing every-frame resolution. This remains a future transfer use case: the first dataset and claim stay wildlife-specific, and robotics results require a separate dataset and evaluation.
