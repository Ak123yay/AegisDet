# Safety Claim Policy

AegisDet-Pro is a research prototype. It must not be described as collision avoidance, guaranteed wildlife warning, law-enforcement surveillance, medical, or other safety-certified detection. Report false negatives, domain shift, lighting limitations, and runtime failure modes. Any future safety-adjacent use requires independent engineering, validation, redundancy, and regulatory analysis.
