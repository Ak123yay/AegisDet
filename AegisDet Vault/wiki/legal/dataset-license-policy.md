# Dataset License Policy

Every image source must have a recorded URL/origin, owner or provider, license/permission, collection date, allowed uses, redistribution status, and attribution requirement. Do not mix “available online” with “permitted for model training.” Private footage requires consent and a retention plan. Dataset cards must distinguish distributable data from data users must acquire independently.
