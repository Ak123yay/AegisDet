# Privacy Policy for Video Data

Collect only footage needed for the wildlife question. Avoid private property, faces, license plates, house interiors, and precise location metadata when unnecessary. Redact or exclude identifiable people/vehicles. Limit access, document retention, and do not upload private footage to third-party services without permission. Public demos should use cleared examples.
