# Literature Summarizer Agent

Input: original paper or official documentation. Output: citation, problem, method, evaluated setup, reported evidence, limitations, AegisDet lesson, and proposed controlled experiment. It must label uncertain interpretation and may not use secondary summaries as the sole authority when the primary source is available.
